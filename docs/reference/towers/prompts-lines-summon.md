# 소환대 계열 프롬프트 (race 3 · 이끼 `#8a9a5b`)

> 근거: [컨셉 §6](../../design/tower-dot-modeling-concept.md).
> **소환된 크리쳐 자체가 타워** — "장비 업그레이드"가 아니라 "소환체 격 상승".
> 통일성은 **돌·룬 재질 + 이끼/룬 발광**으로, 진지성은 발밑 **소환진**으로(티어마다 커짐).
> 가고일(Lv2)은 날개·비행으로 실루엣이 튀지만 **의도된 수용**(원작 풀 구조, 컨셉 D4) —
> 돌+발광 재질과 소환진으로 계열 소속을 읽힌다. 자체 완결형 프롬프트.

---

## 골렘 계열

`summon-mini-golem → summon-gargoyle → summon-magma-golem → summon-rune-golem`
(미니 골렘 → 가고일 → 매그마 골렘 → 룬 골렘)

**계열 표식:** **돌·룬 재질 몸** + 발밑 **소환진**. 이끼 발광 → 용암 발광 → 룬 발광으로 마광이 상승.

```
A horizontal creature evolution sheet: ONE summoned-golem line, four stone creatures in a single
row, tier 1 to tier 4 from left to right, front-facing pose, each standing on a glowing summoning
circle. The creatures grow in scale and power tier by tier; the summoning circle under the feet
grows larger and brighter each tier.

STYLE: chibi low-poly fantasy stone creature, chunky rounded proportions, angular low-poly faceting
with soft cel shading, bold clean readable silhouette. Muted material palette: gray carved stone,
brown rock, dark iron, mossy green and rune glow as accents. Even soft studio lighting from
upper-left. Painted game-asset concept style.

FRAME: flat neutral mid-gray background, NO cast shadow beyond the glowing summoning circle, NO
ground scenery, full creature in frame, identical foot baseline and camera height for every figure,
evenly spaced. Absolutely no text, no letters, no numbers, no UI, no icons, no watermark.

Creature 1 (Lv1, humblest): a small rock golem with moss-covered limbs and a single eye, on a small
faint summoning circle.
Creature 2 (Lv2): a winged stone gargoyle statue with stone wings and horns (a flying silhouette
variant within the line), on a summoning circle.
Creature 3 (Lv3): a large golem with lava light glowing between cracked stone skin, on a larger
brighter circle.
Creature 4 (Lv4, iconic): a rune-engraved refined stone colossus with floating rune-stone joints,
moss replaced by bright rune glow, on a large ornate summoning circle.

CONSISTENCY: every creature reads as STONE + rune/glow material standing on a summoning circle;
the circle and glow grow each tier. Use the accent color ONLY as the moss / rune glow / summoning
circle light: moss green #8a9a5b. Materials stay in the fixed palette; do not tint the whole creature.
```
