# 마법대 계열 프롬프트 (race 2 · 자수정 `#9a6ea6`)

> 근거: [컨셉 §5](../../design/tower-dot-modeling-concept.md).
> 로브 마법사 + 마력 구조물. 재질 기조 = 로브 + 룬 + 부양 결정 + **자수정 마광**.
> **두 계열 구분:** 마법사 = **지팡이 정면 캐스팅**(지팡이 축이 티어를 읽힘),
> 정령술사 = **몸 주위를 도는 원소 궤도 오브**(오브 개수가 티어를 읽힘).
> 마광(발광 픽셀)은 티어마다 늘고 밝아진다 — 상승의 4번째 축. 각 프롬프트는 자체 완결형이다.

---

## 1. 마법사 계열 (splash)

`magic-apprentice-mage → magic-mage → magic-sorcerer → magic-archmage`
(견습 마법사 → 마법사 → 마도사 → 아크메이지)

**계열 표식:** **손에 든 지팡이**. 지팡이가 나무+작은 수정 → 룬 각인 → 큰 결정 → 뜬 결정 클러스터로 진화.

```
A horizontal character evolution sheet: ONE mage, four full-body figures in a single row, tier 1 to
tier 4 from left to right, front-facing casting pose. The SAME character, progressively more
powerful ("gear upgrade" progression), each figure a little taller and denser than the last, with
MORE and BRIGHTER magical glow at higher tiers.

STYLE: chibi low-poly fantasy character, 2–3 heads tall, chunky rounded proportions, angular
low-poly faceting with soft cel shading, bold clean readable silhouette. Muted material palette:
cream and gray robe cloth, brown leather, dark iron, with amethyst magical glow as the light source.
Even soft studio lighting from upper-left. Painted game-asset concept style.

FRAME: flat neutral mid-gray background, NO cast shadow, NO ground line, full body in frame,
identical foot baseline and camera height for every figure, evenly spaced. Absolutely no text,
no letters, no numbers, no UI, no icons, no watermark.

Figure 1 (Lv1, humblest): a short plain cloth robe and hood, a wooden staff with a tiny crystal
tip, poor apprentice look, very faint glow.
Figure 2 (Lv2): a proper robe with shoulder tassels, a rune-engraved staff, one small floating orb.
Figure 3 (Lv3): a decorated robe with a collar, a large crystal-topped staff, three floating runes,
glowing eyes.
Figure 4 (Lv4, iconic): a star-patterned robe with a pointed hood, a floating crystal cluster,
spellcasting fingertips, a rune halo — radiant.

CONSISTENCY: every figure holds a staff as the primary read; the staff evolves wooden-tiny-crystal
-> rune -> large crystal -> floating cluster, and magical glow increases each tier. Use the accent
color ONLY as the magical glow / rune light / robe trim: amethyst #9a6ea6. Materials stay in the
fixed palette; do not tint the whole body.
```

## 2. 정령술사 계열 (splash)

`magic-apprentice-elementalist → magic-elementalist → magic-elemental-sorcerer → magic-grand-elementalist`
(견습 정령술사 → 원소술사 → 원소 마도사 → 대원소술사)

**계열 표식:** **몸 주위를 도는 원소 궤도 오브**. 오브 1 → 2 → 3 → 부양 원소 코어로 개수·격이 상승.
**마법사와의 구분:** 지팡이 축이 아니라 회전 오브가 주 실루엣.

```
A horizontal character evolution sheet: ONE elementalist, four full-body figures in a single row,
tier 1 to tier 4 from left to right, front-facing pose. The SAME character, progressively more
powerful, distinguished by MORE orbiting elemental orbs and brighter glow at higher tiers.

STYLE: chibi low-poly fantasy character, 2–3 heads tall, chunky rounded proportions, angular
low-poly faceting with soft cel shading, bold clean readable silhouette. Muted material palette:
cream and gray robe cloth, brown leather, dark iron, with amethyst magical glow as the light source.
Even soft studio lighting from upper-left. Painted game-asset concept style.

FRAME: flat neutral mid-gray background, NO cast shadow, NO ground line, full body in frame,
identical foot baseline and camera height for every figure, evenly spaced. Absolutely no text,
no letters, no numbers, no UI, no icons, no watermark.

Figure 1 (Lv1, humblest): a modest robe, a single small elemental ember floating above one open hand.
Figure 2 (Lv2): a robe with a totem staff, two elemental orbs orbiting the body.
Figure 3 (Lv3): elemental-rune armor, three orbiting orbs, a small magic circle glowing under the feet.
Figure 4 (Lv4, iconic): a floating elemental core at the chest, wing-shaped mana, a large magic
circle, a horned crown — a grand elementalist.

CONSISTENCY: the primary read is ORBITING elemental orbs around the body (not a staff axis); orb
count grows 1 -> 2 -> 3 -> floating core, and glow increases each tier. Use the accent color ONLY as
the elemental glow / magic circle / robe trim: amethyst #9a6ea6. Materials stay in the fixed palette;
do not tint the whole body.
```
